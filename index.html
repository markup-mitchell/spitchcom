About

I grew up in the early days of home computers, with Spectrums, C64s, BBC micros and Ataris. My focus drifted elsewhere for a few years and the nect time I had cause to use a computer as a university student, everything had changed and MicroSoft ruled the world.

I spent my 20s in a state of permanent war with my PC, furious at its behaviour but powerless to control it. It was too late for me to learn the technical skills required to make my laptop do what I wanted... I would just have to accept defeat.

A few years ago I got a Raspberry Pi for Christmas.
